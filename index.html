Under UFLPA and FEOC rules, Europe and the US now pay a structural premium for traceable, non-Chinese material — creating a durable opening for a neutral, compliant producer.Xitoy global polysiliconning 90% dan ortig'ini nazorat qiladi. Why UzbekistanNega O'zbekiston

The one advantage no competitor can copy.Raqobatchi nusxalay olmaydigan yagona ustunlik.

The benchmark non-Chinese plant — United Solar in Oman — imports its raw material. Uzbekistan's national Critical Minerals list officially recognises silicon and high-purity quartz, with verified deposits (Nurota vein quartz at 98.7% SiO₂). Domestic feedstock + low-cost renewables is the moat.Andaza non-Xitoy zavod — Omondagi United Solar — xomashyoni import qiladi. O'zbekistonning milliy Kritik Minerallar ro'yxati kremniy va yuqori-tozalik kvartsini rasman tan oladi (Nurota tomir kvartsi, 98.7% SiO₂). Ichki xomashyo + arzon renewable — bu himoya devori.
