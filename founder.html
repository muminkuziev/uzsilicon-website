Kuziev Mumin Ulashboevich

Founder and Project Initiator of Uz Silicon TechUz Silicon Tech asoschisi va tashabbuskori

Kuziev Mumin Ulashboevich is the founder and initiator of the Uz Silicon Tech concept — a strategic initiative aimed at establishing a fully integrated silicon value chain in Uzbekistan, including high-purity quartz processing, metallurgical silicon, polysilicon, wafer manufacturing, and related downstream industries. Kuziev Mumin Ulashboevich — Uz Silicon Tech konsepsiyasining asoschisi va tashabbuskori. Bu — O'zbekistonda to'liq integratsiyalashgan kremniy zanjirini barpo etishga qaratilgan strategik tashabbus bo'lib, yuqori tozalikdagi kvarts qayta ishlash, metallurgik silisiy, polysilicon, wafer ishlab chiqarish va tegishli quyi sanoatlarni o'z ichiga oladi.

The concept was developed with the vision of positioning Uzbekistan as a future supplier of traceable, China-independent silicon materials for the global solar and semiconductor industries. Konsepsiya O'zbekistonni global quyosh va yarimo'tkazgich sanoati uchun kuzatiladigan, Xitoyga bog'liq bo'lmagan kremniy materiallari yetkazib beruvchisi sifatida joylashtirish maqsadida ishlab chiqilgan.

Project focusLoyiha yo'nalishlari

  • Development of a national silicon cluster;Milliy kremniy klasterini rivojlantirish;
  • Creation of high-value industrial exports;Yuqori qiymatli sanoat eksportini yaratish;
  • Integration of renewable energy with advanced manufacturing;Qayta tiklanuvchi energiyani ilg'or ishlab chiqarish bilan birlashtirish;
  • Attraction of international strategic partners and investors;Xalqaro strategik sheriklar va investorlarni jalb qilish;
  • Strengthening Uzbekistan's role in global critical minerals supply chains.O'zbekistonning global kritik minerallar ta'minot zanjiridagi rolini mustahkamlash.

The founder's role is to initiate, coordinate, develop, and promote the strategic concept while working with government institutions, investors, industrial partners, and international organizations. Asoschining vazifasi — davlat institutlari, investorlar, sanoat sheriklari va xalqaro tashkilotlar bilan ishlagan holda strategik konsepsiyani tashabbus qilish, muvofiqlashtirish, rivojlantirish va targ'ib qilishdir.
